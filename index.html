Democracy Day In Nigeria

New update about Nigeria Democracy Day,June 12, 2022

Democracy was formerly celebrated on every May 29, a national public holiday in Nigeria.Democracy Day marks the day the military handed over power to an elected civilian government in 1999, marking the beginning of the longest continuous civilian rule since Nigeria's independence from colonial rule in 1960. Not until 2018, when President Muhammadu Buhari led the Federal Government of Nigeria declared June 12 to be the new Democracy Day.

Benefits of the morning sun, July 21, 2021

Vitamin D also provides protection against cancers such as lung, prostate, and skin cancer. It helps in protecting your body from osteoporosis, rickets, and diabetes. In addition, vitamin D is said to help lower blood cholesterol levels thus helping fight heart disease.
